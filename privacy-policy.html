Privacy Policy for Highlite

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of August 27, 2025.

Introduction

Welcome to Highlite, a Chrome extension designed for selecting, highlighting, and copying important information from webpages. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data, ensuring transparency and compliance with your privacy.

Data We Collect

Highlite collects the following data to fulfill its purpose:

  • Text snippets you select, highlight, and copy from webpages.

This data is stored locally on your device using the browser's storage system and is not transmitted to any external servers.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Enable the selection, highlighting, and copying of important information.
  • Store and manage your snippets within the extension for easy access.

No data is used for advertising, analytics, or third-party sharing.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device via Chrome's local storage.
  • We do not access, process, or store data on remote servers.
  • Data security is maintained by the browser's storage mechanisms; we recommend keeping your browser updated.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties under any circumstances.

Your Choices

  • You control what text you select and copy into Highlite.
  • You can delete snippets directly within the extension at any time.
  • To clear all data, use your browser's storage clearing options.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in functionality. Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.
